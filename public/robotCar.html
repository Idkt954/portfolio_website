smart controlled robot car

Introduction

    This project was actually inspired by Robotics workshop offered by KMITL in K-Engineering World Tour and Workshop 2023. At first, I wanted to make this project involved with AI as well. I ran into a channel on YouTube called "DroneBot Workshop", he teaches different kind of boards and how they work. In one of his videos, he used "ESP32-cam" with "Edge Impulse" to process object detection. As I watched the video, an idea popped into my head that I could use it with my project. I wanted to do hand gestures in order to make the car moves and do several functions. Unfortunately, when I tested the ESP32-cam it gives a really bad delay maybe because of the wifi connection and other factors. Therefore, I replanned the project and here it is!
